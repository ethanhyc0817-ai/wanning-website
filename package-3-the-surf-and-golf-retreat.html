The Trip

Two sports. One island. Zero logistics.

Surf in the morning. Tee off in the afternoon. Hainan is one of the only places in the world where you can fit world-class surfing and championship golf into a single day — without a long drive or a packed schedule.

Mission Hills, Shenzhou Peninsula, and a few quieter local courses sit within easy reach of the wave pool. Tiger Woods, Rory McIlroy, Greg Norman, Nick Faldo, and Gary Player have all played these fairways. Most golfers have watched a tournament shot from here without realizing where they were looking.

It’s the trip for the friend group, the couple, or the traveler who wants both halves of the day to count.

The Surf

Wave pool first. Natural surf when it’s on.

PerfectSwell® is the anchor of the trip. Twelve or more sessions over the week, matched to your level — from beginner-friendly waves to faster open-face sections and barrel settings. Coaching, guiding, and video feedback can be built in.

When the ocean turns on, we add a natural surf window along Hainan’s coast — warm water, beach and reef bottom, almost no crowd. Optional, weather-dependent, and worth it when it lines up.

The Golf

Three rounds. Two of the best courses in China.

Hainan is China’s tropical golf capital. The fairways are warm year-round, the water hazards are real ocean, and the courses have hosted the HSBC Champions, the Mission Hills World Cup, and the Volvo China Open.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Which courses do we play?

We match the courses to your group and the time of year — usually two championship layouts (Mission Hills, Shenzhou Peninsula, or similar) plus one quieter local round. Tee times, caddies, and transport are all arranged.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

No. Club rental is available at every course we play. If you’d rather bring your own, we’ll handle storage between rounds.

What if some of us only surf, or only play golf?

Mixed groups are common. Surf-only and golf-only travelers can swap any day for an extra session, a recovery day, or an island day at no extra cost.

Do I need surf experience?

No. The pool has beginner-friendly settings and coaching options. We’ll match sessions to your level.

Where should I fly into?

Fly into Haikou Meilan International Airport (HAK). We arrange the transfer to Wanning.

When is the best time to come?

October to April. Cooler, drier, and the best conditions for both surf and golf. The wave pool and the courses operate year-round, so the trip can still work outside that window.
