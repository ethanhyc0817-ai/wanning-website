The Trip

Two tiers. Premium 7N or Luxury 11N.

Both tiers anchor on the same two facts: PerfectSwell® (the only American Wave Machines pool in Asia, the same tech behind Waco) and championship golf on courses that have hosted the HSBC Champions and the Volvo China Open. The Dunes at Shenzhou is Tom Weiskopf’s only Asian links design, ranked #2 in China. Mission Hills Blackstone is #1 in China, World Top 100 — Tiger and Rory have played it head-to-head.

Premium is single-region: 5N at the Wave Pool Resort in Wanning, 2N at the Ritz-Carlton Haikou, two rounds at The Dunes and one at Blackstone. Luxury adds a Sanya leg: 4N in the Wave Pool Resort Owner’s Suite, 5N at Rosewood Sanya, 2N at the Ritz-Carlton Haikou Golf Suite — with a private yacht charter, a cultural day, drone coverage, and the Lu Hui Tou round in Sanya replacing one of the Wanning rounds.

Couples, friend groups, and mixed-sport travellers. Surf level and golf handicap mix — we shape both around the group.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Which courses do we play?

Premium: 2× The Dunes at Shenzhou Peninsula (Wanning) + 1× Mission Hills Blackstone (Haikou). Luxury: 1× The Dunes + 1× Lu Hui Tou (Sanya) + 1× Mission Hills Blackstone. Tee times, charter transport, and caddies all arranged.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

Club rental is available at every course. It’s an add-on rather than bundled — tell us and we’ll quote it. If you’d rather bring your own, we’ll handle transport and storage between rounds.

What if some of us only surf, or only play golf?

Common. The non-surfer or non-golfer keeps the room, breakfast, transfers, and trip support — we strip their surf or golf inclusions at cost. Their free time is their own to fill (we don’t plan or quote leisure activities for non-surfing companions).

Do I need surf experience?

No. PerfectSwell® dials from soft beginner waves to clean open faces — sessions are matched to your level.

Lefts or rights?

The pool alternates daily — lefts on odd calendar days, rights on even. Once dates lock, we’ll tell you exactly which surf mornings are which.

Where should I fly into and out of?

In via Haikou Meilan (HAK), out via HAK. On Luxury, in via HAK or Sanya Phoenix (SYX), out via HAK after the final Haikou nights. All transfers included.
