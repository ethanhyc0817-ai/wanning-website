## FAQ

**Do I need a visa?**
Probably not. Hainan offers 30-day visa-free entry for passport holders from the US, UK, EU, Australia, Canada, and 50+ other countries. We'll walk you through it.

**How do I get to Wanning?**
Fly into Sanya Phoenix International (SYX). We pick you up. Sanya connects to Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Tokyo, Seoul, and direct from a few European cities seasonally.

**Can I bring my own clubs?**
Yes. We'll handle the transfer from airport to villa to course and back. You can also leave them with us between rounds.

**Is the course walkable?**
Carts are standard and included. Walking is allowed for some rounds — ask if you prefer that.

**What if my partner doesn't surf or golf?**
We've built this trip with that in mind. Non-playing partners get full access to spa, beach days, cultural day trips, and the villa. Pricing is the same.

**What if the pool is down for maintenance?**
We'll know months ahead. If a maintenance window clashes with your dates, we move dates or shift to natural surf and exploration days — your call.

**Three rounds feels light. Can I add more?**
Yes. Many guests add a 4th or 5th round at the course rate. Tell us when you book.

**What's the weather like?**
October to April is the season for both surf and golf: 22–28°C (72–82°F), low humidity, light breeze. Avoid May–September: hot, rainy, and uncomfortable for golf even if the surf is still good.
