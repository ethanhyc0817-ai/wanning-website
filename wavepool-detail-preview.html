Sessions

Pick your session.

Whether it's your first time standing up or your thousandth barrel, there's a session dialed in for you.

Learn to Surf

Your first wave, ever. Soft boards, a coach in the water, and whitewater waves designed to get you standing up on day one.

Beginner

Past whitewater and ready to ride the shoulder. Mellow, reformed waves to practice your pop-up, trim, and first turns.

Intermediate

Clean, open-face walls you can actually draw a line on. The best wave in the pool for building speed, flow, and real turns.

Advanced

Faster, punchier, more sections. Uncrowded waves to link turns, commit to the lip, and push every session further than the last.

Barrel Session

Hollow, heavy, and on demand. The session everyone comes for — get tubed more times in an hour than most surfers get in a lifetime.

Air Session

Ramp section on repeat. Punt airs, work rotations, and treat the pool like your own private launch pad.

Private Session

Book the whole pool. Custom wave settings, your crew only — perfect for film shoots, family trips, or a dream session dialed to you.

FAQ

Things surfers ask.

Do I need to know how to surf?

No. The pool runs beginner sessions on whitewater and reform waves — you'll be up and riding on your first day with coaching.

What's the minimum age?

Kids' programs start at age 6. [确认实际年龄下限]

How many waves do I get per session?

Session volume depends on the wave type and group size — typically 10–15 waves per 60-minute session. [需要准确数字]

Do I need to bring my own board?

Not required. The on-site surf shop has quality rental boards for every level and every wave type.

Can non-surfers watch?

Yes — comfortable viewing decks, a cafe, and a clubhouse. Spectators welcome and free.

What's the water temperature?

Warm year-round. Most surfers wear boardshorts or a spring suit even in winter. [需要确认]

Can I book just the pool, without a hotel package?

Yes. Standalone session booking is available. Trip packages combine the pool with accommodation, transfers, and support.
