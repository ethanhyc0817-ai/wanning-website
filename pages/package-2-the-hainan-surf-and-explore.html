The Trip

Four surf mornings. Two private culture days. The slow Hainan week.

5 nights at the Wave Pool Resort in Wanning, 2 nights at a 5-star hotel on Sanya Bay. Four surf mornings at PerfectSwell® (two sessions each), a full-day private charter into the Limu highlands to Areca Manor — the Li & Miao villages most travellers never see — and a full-day Sanya charter to Yalong Bay Forest Park and the Nanshan Guanyin, plus an evening at a Sanya night market.

Less surf volume than Wave Pool Escape, more local content. The middle gear.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Do I need surf experience?

No. PerfectSwell® dials from soft beginner waves to clean open faces — the trip’s lighter surf volume (2 sessions per surf morning, not every day) suits a learning trip well.

What if my partner doesn’t surf?

A non-surfing companion is welcome along — they keep the room, breakfast, transfers, and trip support. The two culture days run for everyone. Their free pool mornings are simply their own to fill.

Where should I fly into and out of?

Easiest: in via Haikou Meilan (HAK) or Sanya Phoenix (SYX), out via Sanya Phoenix (SYX). Private transfer to Wanning included on arrival; private transfer to SYX on departure included.

Lefts or rights?

The pool alternates daily — lefts on odd calendar days, rights on even. Once dates lock, we’ll tell you exactly which mornings are which.

Can the culture days change?

The standard build is Areca Manor + the Sanya day. We can swap or add — Mission Hills Hot Spring, Haikou City Tour, a yacht charter — on request, priced as add-ons.
