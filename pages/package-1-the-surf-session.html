FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Do I need surf experience?

No. PerfectSwell® dials from soft, beginner-friendly waves up to steeper open faces. Sessions are matched to your level — first-timers and experienced surfers both fit.

Can I add more sessions?

Yes — US$80 per extra session, on request. The package runs 2/day for the couple as standard.

Do I need to bring a board?

No. Premium board rental is included for both of you, full trip. Boards matched to your level and the day’s wave.

Lefts or rights?

The pool alternates daily — lefts on odd calendar days, rights on even. Once dates lock, we’ll tell you exactly which days are which.

Where should I fly into?

Haikou Meilan (HAK) is the usual entry point; Sanya Phoenix (SYX) also works. Round-trip Premium Car transfer to the Surf Pool Resort in Wanning is included from either.
