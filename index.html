How It Works

PerfectSwell® uses a patented air-pressure system to replicate natural ocean swells. Choose your session level, paddle out, and surf — waves up to 2.7 meters high, with ride times up to 18 seconds. No waiting for swell. No fighting crowds.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to bring my own surfboard?

Not at all. The on-site surf shop has quality boards for every level, and rentals are available by the session or the trip. Many guests travel light and rent here.

What level do I need to be?

The PerfectSwell® pool caters to all levels — from absolute beginners to advanced competitive surfers. Waves are adjusted per session, so you'll always be in appropriate conditions.

How long is a session?

Sessions typically run 45–60 minutes depending on wave type and group size. You'll get plenty of waves in every session.

Can spectators watch?

Absolutely. The pool has comfortable viewing areas, a café, and a clubhouse where non-surfers can relax and enjoy the atmosphere.

Can kids surf here?

Yes. Beginner-friendly waves and coaching sessions are suitable for children. It's a safe, controlled environment ideal for young surfers.

Ready to ride?

Book your PerfectSwell® sessions as part of a trip package or ask about standalone access.

Tropical China,
like nothing you'd expect.

Waterfalls, coffee plantations, fire shows, night markets, and empty coastlines. A side of China most travelers never see.

More than a wave pool destination.

Hainan is China's tropical island province — warm year-round, culturally rich, and beautifully undiscovered by international travelers. The wave pool is why you come. Hainan is why you stay longer than you planned.

Coffee Plantations

Waterfalls

Night Entertainment

Night Markets

Tropical flavors & local life

Wanning

Surf Town Atmosphere

The wave pool's home town. A laid-back coastal community with local surf shops, cafés, and a growing surf culture that feels genuine and welcoming.

Xinglong

Hot Springs & Coffee Culture

Just minutes away, Xinglong is known for natural hot springs, lush tropical gardens, and a unique Southeast Asian-influenced cuisine brought by returning overseas Chinese.

Nature

Mountains, Rivers & Coastline

Hainan's interior is mountainous and wild. Rivers carve through palm forests. The coastline alternates between dramatic cliffs and long sandy beaches.

Culture & Food

Night Markets & Local Cuisine

Fresh seafood, tropical fruit, local specialties, chocolate-making experiences, and fire performances. Immersive, delicious, and endlessly photogenic.

For Non-Surfers

Not everyone has to surf.

While the surfer in your group is in the pool, there's a full tropical island to explore. Spa treatments, nature hikes, river excursions, cooking experiences, markets, and the beach club.

We design itineraries where surfers and non-surfers can split during the day and come back together for incredible shared experiences.

Getting to Hainan is easier than you think. Here's what to expect.

Getting There

How to Reach Hainan

Fly into Sanya Phoenix International Airport (SYX) or Haikou Meilan (HAK). Direct flights from major Asian hubs. We handle your transfer to the resort — approximately 90 minutes of scenic coastal driving.

Accommodation

Where You'll Stay

Premium rooms at the Riyue Bay Surf Resort. Choose between modern hotel rooms with mountain views or designer boutique rooms. All within walking distance of the PerfectSwell® pool.

Language

English Support

Full English-speaking trip coordination from booking to checkout. Your guide, transfers, and all communications are in English. We're available via WhatsApp throughout your stay.

Transport

Getting Around

All packages include airport transfers and resort transport. For exploration days, we arrange guided transport to Hainan's best spots. No local transit to figure out.

Gear

Board Rentals & Surf Shop

Travel light. The on-site surf shop carries all major brands, and quality board rentals are available for every session. Bring your own if you prefer — but you don't have to.

Best Time to Visit

When to Come

The PerfectSwell® pool operates year-round. Hainan is tropical — warm in every season. October through April offers the most comfortable weather and best natural surf conditions.

FAQ

Trip questions, answered.

Do I need a visa to visit China?

Hainan offers visa-free entry for visitors from many countries for stays up to 30 days. We'll help you confirm your eligibility during trip planning.

Is it worth flying to China for this?

This is not just a wave pool — it's a full tropical destination trip. PerfectSwell® waves, premium accommodation, unique cultural experiences, and an island most travelers have never explored. Many guests tell us it was their most surprising and memorable surf trip.

What if I don't speak Chinese?

We handle everything in English. Your trip coordinator, airport pickup, and all communications are in English. We're also available via WhatsApp throughout your stay.

What if only one person in our group surfs?

Our packages are designed for exactly this. While the surfer is in the pool, non-surfers can enjoy the hotel pool, beach club, spa, Hainan exploration, or simply relax at the recovery suite.

Can the trip be customized?

Absolutely. Every trip can be tailored to your dates, group size, interests, and budget.

Is it family-friendly?

Very much so. The wave pool offers kid-friendly sessions, the resort is comfortable for families, and Hainan has plenty of activities for all ages.
