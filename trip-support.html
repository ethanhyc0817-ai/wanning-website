On Site · Board Rentals

Board Rentals

Travel light. Surf the right board.

Choose from shortboards, longboards, mid-lengths, and soft tops matched to your level, session type, and goals. Whether you’re learning, working on turns, or chasing your first barrel, we’ll help you get on the right equipment.

  • Soft tops for beginners
  • Longboards and mid-lengths
  • Performance shortboards
  • Boards matched to wave settings
  • Rental support before your session

Recovery & Wellness

Surf hard. Recover properly.

Located about 20 minutes from the wave pool, the recovery space gives you a proper reset between surf days — sauna, ice bath, Jacuzzi, and optional massage depending on availability.

It’s not inside the wave pool grounds, but it can be built into your trip flow for heavier surf days, longer stays, or premium package upgrades.

  • About 20 minutes from the wave pool
  • Sauna
  • Ice bath
  • Jacuzzi
  • Post-session recovery
  • Optional massage if available
  • Available with selected packages or add-ons

FAQ

Quick answers.

Do I need to bring my own board?

No. Board rentals are available, with options for different levels and wave types.

Can non-surfers use the café?

Yes. The café is useful for surfers, partners, friends, and family members watching or waiting between sessions.

Is recovery located inside the wave pool grounds?

No. Recovery & Wellness is located about 20 minutes from the wave pool. It can be built into selected packages or added depending on your trip plan.

Can I buy surf gear on site?

Yes. The surf shop carries session essentials and selected surf gear, so you don’t have to overpack.

Tell us your dates, group size, and surf level. We’ll help you plan the right package, board setup, recovery flow, and trip support.
