The Trip

You don't have a week. You have a long weekend — and you want to actually surf.

Not paddle out, sit, wait, paddle back in for the one you missed. The Wave Pool Escape is built for that.

Three nights in a premium villa minutes from the world's only PerfectSwell® pool in China. Six guaranteed sessions, every wave coached and filmed, every hour you're awake either in the water or recovering for it. We handle the airport, the food, the boards, the rest. You handle the surfing.

It's for the surfer who wants more reps than a coastal trip can give them — without the noise of a full week away.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Do I need a visa?

Probably not. Hainan offers 30-day visa-free entry for passport holders from the US, UK, EU, Australia, Canada, and 50+ other countries. We'll walk you through it.

How do I get there?

Fly into Sanya Phoenix International (SYX). We pick you up. Sanya connects to Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Tokyo, Seoul — and a few European cities seasonally.

I've never surfed. Can I still come?

Yes. The wave pool is built for first-timers. Most beginners are riding green waves by their third session, which on this trip is your second day.

What if the pool is down for maintenance?

We'll know months ahead. If a maintenance window clashes with your dates, we move you, refund you, or shift to natural surf at Riyue Bay — your choice.

How much English is spoken locally?

Off-property, very little. Your whole trip is run in English by us. That's why we're useful.

Can I do more sessions?

Yes. Up to 3 a day. Tell us before you arrive so we can book the slots and make sure you've got the recovery time you need.

What's the weather like?

The pool runs year-round. October to April is our peak season for surrounding ocean conditions: 22–28°C (72–82°F), warm water, low humidity. May to September is rainier but still surfable, and quieter for crowds.
