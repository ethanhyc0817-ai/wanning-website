The Trip

A quick escape built around one thing: surfing more.

No waiting for swell. No crowded lineup. No wasted travel days. Just consistent PerfectSwell® sessions, a comfortable stay steps from the pool, and everything handled before you arrive.

Come for a long weekend, get real reps, recover properly, and leave surfing better than when you landed.

Sample Itinerary

4 days, 3 nights.

A baseline plan. We flex it around your level, your group, and the schedule you fly in on.

Day 01
Arrival & Settle In

Airport pickup, transfer to Wanning, check-in, board fitting, resort orientation, and a relaxed first night.

Day 02
Two Pool Sessions

Morning PerfectSwell® session, coaching or video review, lunch, rest, and a second session in the afternoon.

Day 03
Progression Day

Another full surf day built around your level — first rides, turns, speed, barrels, or simply more reps. Optional recovery session after surfing.

Day 04
Final Surf & Departure

Optional morning session depending on your flight time, brunch, checkout, and airport transfer.

The Surf

Real waves. On demand. Built around you.

Six sessions are included as standard, with the option to add more if you want a heavier surf block.

Your sessions are matched to your level — from beginner-friendly waves to faster open-face sections and barrel settings. Coaching, guiding, and video feedback can be built into the trip depending on your goals.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Do I need surf experience?

No. The pool has beginner-friendly waves and coaching options. We'll match sessions to your level.

Can I add more sessions?

Yes. Six sessions are included as the base structure, and extra sessions can be added depending on availability and recovery time.

Do I need to bring a board?

No. Board rental is included, with options for different levels and wave types.

Where should I fly into?

Fly into Haikou Meilan International Airport (HAK). We arrange the transfer to Wanning.
