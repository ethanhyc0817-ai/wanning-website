# Surf & Golf — Hainan Itinerary

Two trip lengths, one philosophy: every day either has waves, a championship golf course, or both. PerfectSwell® Hainan is the only Kelly Slater-tech surf pool in Asia. The Dunes at Shenzhou Peninsula is China's #2 golf course (Tom Weiskopf). Mission Hills Blackstone is China's #1, and a World Top 100. The Luxury tier extends the journey to Sanya — Rosewood beachfront, a private yacht, Lu Hui Tou Golf Club, and cultural Hainan.

All trips include English-language trip support throughout. Pricing is per person, couple sharing one room.

---

## PREMIUM Surf & Golf — 7 nights / 8 days

5 nights Wanning · 2 nights Haikou · 3 surf sessions · 3 golf rounds · 2 cities

### Day 1 — Arrival → Wanning

Private Premium Car round-trip transfer from Sanya Phoenix International (≈1.5 hrs) or Haikou Meilan International (≈2 hrs). Check in to your **Ocean & Pool View Room at the Surf Pool Resort**. Walk the property, settle into the surf lineup view from your balcony. Casual dinner on site.

### Day 2 — Wanning · Surf 1

Morning **PerfectSwell® session #1** at the surf pool. Setting depends on the calendar — odd days run lefts, even days run rights. Photo coverage included during your session.

Free afternoon — beach, pool, recovery.

### Day 3 — Wanning · Golf at The Dunes (Round 1)

Private charter car to **The Dunes at Shenzhou Peninsula** (≈30 min). 18 holes at China's #2 course — Tom Weiskopf links design carved through coastal forest and dune. Caddy included. Return to the resort late afternoon.

### Day 4 — Wanning · Surf 2

**PerfectSwell® session #2**. Photo coverage included.

Free afternoon.

### Day 5 — Wanning · Golf at The Dunes (Round 2)

Private charter car back to **The Dunes**. 18 holes — second look at the course you just learned, this time with reads on the wind and greens. Caddy included.

### Day 6 — Wanning → Haikou

Morning **PerfectSwell® session #3** at the surf pool — final wave day.

Afternoon charter car to Haikou (≈2 hrs). Check in to your **Deluxe Golf Ocean View Room at the Ritz-Carlton Haikou** at Mission Hills.

### Day 7 — Haikou · Golf at Mission Hills Blackstone (Round 3)

Round 3 at **Mission Hills Blackstone Course** — #1 in China, World Top 100, hosted Tiger Woods × Rory McIlroy. The volcanic lava-rock terrain is unlike anything else in Asian golf. Caddy included.

### Day 8 — Haikou City Tour → Departure

Morning city tour with an **English-speaking guide**:

- **Qilou Old Street (骑楼老街)** — Haikou's historic arcaded shopping street, Southeast-Asian-influenced architecture, coffee shops and old apothecaries.
- **Wormhole Library / Cloud Pavilion (云洞图书馆)** — MAD Architects' organic concrete landmark on the Haikou waterfront. Photogenic stop.

Afternoon Sedan transfer to Haikou Meilan Intl.

## LUXURY Surf & Golf — 11 nights / 12 days

4 nights Wanning · 5 nights Sanya · 2 nights Haikou · 3 surf sessions · 3 golf rounds · yacht charter · 3 cities

### Day 1 — Arrival → Wanning

Private Executive Van transfer from Sanya Phoenix International or Haikou Meilan International. Check in to your **Owner's Suite at the Surf Pool Resort** — the top-tier accommodation inside the surf complex.

### Day 2 — Wanning · Surf 1 + Photo

Morning **PerfectSwell® session #1**. Premium board rental for the trip — pick what matches the day's setting. Drone photography session in the afternoon.

### Day 3 — Wanning · Surf 2

**PerfectSwell® session #2**. Drone coverage during your session.

Free afternoon — beach, hotel pool, or arrange the optional Private Pool Buyout (see add-ons).

### Day 4 — Wanning · Golf at The Dunes

Private charter car to **The Dunes at Shenzhou Peninsula**. 18 holes at China's #2 — coastal links, Tom Weiskopf, sea breeze. Caddy included.

### Day 5 — Wanning → Sanya

Morning **PerfectSwell® session #3** — last waves before heading south.

Afternoon charter transfer Wanning → Sanya (≈3 hrs along the coastal highway). Check in to your **Deluxe Ocean View Room at Rosewood Sanya (瑰丽酒店)** — direct beachfront on Haitang Bay.

### Day 6 — Sanya · Yacht Charter

Full-day **private yacht charter** out of Sanya Bay or Yalong Bay with an English-speaking guide. Cruise, swim, paddle, lunch on board. Drone coverage of the yacht and coastline.

### Day 7 — Sanya · Areca Manor + Night Market

Private charter car + English-speaking guide for a full Hainan-culture day.

- **Areca Manor / Binglanggu (槟榔谷)** — Li and Miao minority cultural park inland from Sanya. Traditional housing, weaving, dance performances, betel-nut history. (Admission ticket payable on-site or via add-on — see notes.)
- Evening: **Sanya night market (夜市)** — Lin Dan / Di Yi Shi Chang area. Tropical fruit, fresh seafood by the kilo, local Hainanese street food.

### Day 8 — Sanya · Golf at Lu Hui Tou

Private charter car to **Lu Hui Tou Golf Club** for Round 2. Mountain-and-sea views over Sanya Bay. Caddy included.

### Day 9 — Sanya · Free Day

Rest day. Beach, spa, pool — or arrange an on-request add-on (additional yacht day, scuba, hot springs, etc.).

### Day 10 — Sanya → Haikou

Private charter car Sanya → Haikou (≈3 hrs). Check in to your **Golf Suite at the Ritz-Carlton Haikou** at Mission Hills.

### Day 11 — Haikou · Golf at Mission Hills Blackstone

Round 3 at **Mission Hills Blackstone Course** — #1 in China, World Top 100, the Tiger × Rory venue. Caddy included.

Evening photo session at the resort.

### Day 12 — Haikou City Tour → Departure

Morning city tour with **English-speaking guide**:

- **Qilou Old Street (骑楼老街)** — Haikou's historic arcaded shopping street.
- **Wormhole Library / Cloud Pavilion (云洞图书馆)** — MAD Architects' waterfront landmark.

Executive Van transfer to Haikou Meilan Intl.
