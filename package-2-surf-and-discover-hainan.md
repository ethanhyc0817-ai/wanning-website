## THE PITCH

Hainan is China's southern edge — a tropical island most Western surfers have never heard of, with a coastline that feels closer to Bali in 1995 than to anywhere in 2026. We built this trip because we think that's worth showing you.

Your mornings are spent in the water. We run multiple sessions a day at the PerfectSwell® wave pool in Wanning — the same technology Kelly Slater's team helped pioneer — so whether you're standing up for the first time or working on your backhand snap, you get the exact wave you need, on demand. Your afternoons are yours: the coastline, the food, the old villages, the jungle inland. We drive, we translate, we introduce you to the right people, and we stay out of your way.

It's for surfers who want to come home better than they left — and who want to see a part of China that isn't on anyone's feed yet.

## SAMPLE ITINERARY (7-day version)

**Day 1 — Arrival**
We pick you up at Sanya Phoenix International (SYX) and drive you up the coast to Wanning. Villa check-in, welcome dinner at a Hakka kitchen tucked into the mountains, early night.

**Day 2 — Into the Pool**
Two PerfectSwell® sessions with video breakdown between them. Afternoon at the beach, getting your legs back. Dinner on the water in Shimei Bay.

**Day 3 — The Coast**
Morning session, then a day driving the coastline south: Sun & Moon Bay, stops at black-sand beaches most locals haven't been to, lunch at a fishing harbor where the boats are still coming in. Back for a golden-hour session.

We trade the coast for the interior — Hainan's tropical rainforest, a Li minority village, a waterfall swim, a tea ceremony at a small plantation. Back to the villa by dinner.

Double session day. Morning and afternoon in the pool, with video coaching in between. Optional third session at dusk if you've got it in you. Dinner at a Sanya night market.

When conditions line up (October – April, most swells), we chase a real break on Hainan's east coast. Crew lunch at a seafood shack. Back to the villa for a recovery sauna and film night.

**Day 7 — Goodbye**
One last session if you want it. Brunch at the villa. Transfer to SYX.

## THE SURF

**The Wave Pool**
PerfectSwell® technology, built by American Wave Machines — the same system behind Waco, Praia da Grama, and Pulsamos. It's not a standing wave. It's a *real* wave, breaking down the line, customizable in size and shape. Beginners get soft, shoulder-high rollers they can stand on. Advanced surfers get chest-to-overhead rights and lefts with real sections. No paddling for scraps. No crowds. The wave you need, every time.

**Coaching**
Every session is coached. Video on every wave, reviewed with you in under an hour. Your coach is a working pro with instruction experience — not a lifeguard with a clipboard. We cap at two students per coach so you actually get feedback.

**Natural Surf**
When the swell is on (October through April, sweet spot December – March), we drive to Hainan's real breaks — Riyue Bay, Sun & Moon Bay, and a few spots we'll show you but won't name online. Shoulder-high-plus beach breaks, warm water year-round, reef and sand bottom. Not Mentawai, but far better than you've been told.

## FAQ

**Do I need a visa?**
Probably not. Hainan offers 30-day visa-free entry for passport holders from the US, UK, EU, Australia, Canada, and 50+ other countries. We'll walk you through it.

**How do I get to Wanning?**
Fly into Sanya Phoenix International (SYX). We pick you up. Sanya connects to Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Tokyo, Seoul, and direct from a few European cities seasonally.

**How much English is spoken?**
Your whole trip is run in English by us. Off-property, most locals don't speak English — which is part of why having us makes this trip work.

**What if I've never surfed before?**
Perfect. The wave pool is built for this. Most guests are standing and riding green waves by their third session.

**What if the pool is down for maintenance?**
We'll know months ahead. If a scheduled maintenance window clashes with your dates, we either move dates or lean into more natural surf and exploration days — your call.

**What's the weather like?**
October to April is the season: 22–28°C (72–82°F), warm water, low humidity. May to September is rainier, still surfable, better for avoiding crowds.

**Can my non-surfing partner join?**
Yes. We've built this so non-surfers stay busy — spa, beach, day trips with the cultural crew. Pricing is the same.
