## FAQ

**Do I need a visa?**
Probably not. Hainan offers 30-day visa-free entry for passport holders from the US, UK, EU, Australia, Canada, and 50+ other countries. We'll walk you through it.

**How do I get there?**
Fly into Sanya Phoenix International (SYX). We pick you up. Sanya connects to Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Tokyo, Seoul — and a few European cities seasonally.

**I've never surfed. Can I still come?**
Yes. The wave pool is built for first-timers. Most beginners are riding green waves by their third session, which on this trip is your second day.

**What if the pool is down for maintenance?**
We'll know months ahead. If a maintenance window clashes with your dates, we move you, refund you, or shift to natural surf — your choice.

**How much English is spoken locally?**
Off-property, very little. That's why we're useful.

**Can I do more sessions?**
Yes. Up to 3 a day. Tell us before you arrive so we can book the slots.
