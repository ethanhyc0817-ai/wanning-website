The Property

A quiet, considered stay by the sea.

The Designer Room and Designer Suite are the work of Shuhei Aoyama — one of Japan's most celebrated architects working in China. Natural timber, warm stone, and clean lines: a space built to slow you down between sessions.

Beyond the designer rooms, the property also offers Ocean & Pool View Rooms looking straight onto the wave pool, and the Owner's Suite — a residence-style stay for longer trips or small groups.
